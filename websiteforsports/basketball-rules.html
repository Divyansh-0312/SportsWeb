Basketball Rules

General Rules

  • The game consists of four quarters of 10 minutes each.
  • Each team has five players on the court.
  • The ball can be advanced by dribbling or passing.
  • Scoring is achieved by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop.

Fouls

  • Personal fouls are called for illegal physical contact.
  • Accumulating five personal fouls will result in disqualification.
  • Technical fouls can be assessed for unsportsmanlike conduct.

Free Throws

  • A free throw is awarded after certain fouls.
  • The shooter must remain behind the free-throw line.
