Football Rules

General Rules

  • A team consists of 11 players including the goalkeeper.
  • Each match lasts 90 minutes, divided into two halves.
  • Offside rules apply as per standard regulations.

Fouls and Free Kicks

  • Fouls may result in direct or indirect free kicks.
  • Penalty kicks are awarded for fouls in the penalty area.
