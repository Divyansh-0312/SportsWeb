Rule Book

General Rules

  • All participants must register before the event.
  • Each team must have a designated captain.
  • Players must wear appropriate sports attire.
  • Respect for referees and officials is mandatory.

Game-Specific Rules

Basketball

  • The game consists of four quarters of 10 minutes each.
  • Each team can have a maximum of 5 players on the court.
  • Fouls will result in free throws.

Football

  • The match duration is 90 minutes, split into two halves.
  • A team consists of 11 players including the goalkeeper.
  • Offside rules apply as per standard regulations.

Athletics

  • Events include sprints, relays, and long-distance races.
  • All participants must follow lane regulations.
  • False starts will result in disqualification.

Swimming

  • Swimmers must start from the block as instructed.
  • Each race will have specific strokes as per event regulations.
  • Disqualification occurs for rule violations during the race.
